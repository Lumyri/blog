Uni-Face

My Uni-Face is Melanie Kolb. She's 23 and is originally from Wangen, but moved to Weingarten due to her studying at RWU. She started studying Business Administration Studies this year and is therefore in the first semester. Before that she already completed an apprenticeship as an industrial clerk and worked in that field for a year. Since she moved to Weingarten, not far away from the university, you can always see her riding her bike, which became kind of a hobby to her then. In her freetime she also likes to read many different books, best in her lovely home on her favorite spot on the couch. Another hobby of hers is cooking and especially baking. No matter if a fancy cake or yummie muffins, Melli can do anything!
