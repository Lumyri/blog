Uni-Face

In this series you can see a student from my university. He is studying Electrical and Computer Engineering in his first year now. After completing an apprenticeship as an electronic technician he decided to study at his 24th. The Photos are not only showing him but also capturing him with his surroundings. As a result they are giving us an insight in his life and we get to know his personality.
A big thanks goes to him for being my model for the pictures!
